HACSA - Tech4Girls

A women-led, global, non-profit, non-partisan, non-governmental, civil society organisation and charity with its headquarters in Ghana, the Heritage and Cultural Society of Africa (HACSA) Foundation is registered as a 501(c)(3) organisation in the Caribbean and the United States. Through collaboration, advocacy, education, and innovation, we promote social impact and transformation within the framework of the Sustainable Development Goals of the United Nations.

Tech4Girls is a practical learning initiative that helps women and girls grow their careers by teaching them digital skills including coding, AI, and ICT. With the help of this program, African women and girls will have the chance to learn about technology and develop the skills necessary for careers in STEM fields. Soft skills like leadership, emotional intelligence, and entrepreneurship are also taught to them.
